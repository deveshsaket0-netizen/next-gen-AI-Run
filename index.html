//// FAQ

Common inquiries

What is Armory and who is it for?

Armory is an AI-native automation platform for engineering and operations teams. It lets you design, deploy, and monitor autonomous AI agents that handle complex workflows at enterprise scale — without writing infrastructure code.

Do I need to know how to code to use Armory?

No. The visual workflow builder lets you construct multi-agent pipelines using a drag-and-drop canvas. Developers can also access the full API for programmatic control.

How does the pricing work?

We offer three tiers — Starter, Pro, and Enterprise — billed monthly or annually (20% discount). Pricing adjusts dynamically across INR, USD, and EUR based on regional rates.

What's the uptime guarantee?

Enterprise plans include a 99.98% uptime SLA with automatic failover and rollback. All plans benefit from our globally distributed infrastructure with 12ms average latency.

Can I deploy Armory on-premise?

Yes. Enterprise plans support on-premise and private cloud deployments. Contact our team to discuss your infrastructure requirements.
